These days we hear more and more about `serverless architecture`, but I think there is a lot of hype around the term. In this article I will try to give a concise description on what that term mean and will provide an implementation example by making use of AWS services.

## What does `serverless` mean?

Before defining what `serverless` actually mean, let's first understand what are the primary types of services offered by cloud computing:
- **Infrastructure as a Service (Iaas).**
- **Platform as a Service (PaaS).**
- **Software as a Service (SaaS).**
